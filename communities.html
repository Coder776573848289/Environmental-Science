Human Communities & Environment

Pandemics

  • Positive: Reduced emissions during COVID-19 lockdown
  • Negative: Biomedical waste increased by ~500 tons/day in India (2020)

Natural Disasters

  • Floods: 2022 Assam floods displaced 4.8 million people
  • Cyclones: Odisha highly vulnerable (1–2 cyclones/year)
  • Earthquakes: Himalayan belt – high seismic risk

Population Growth

  • India: Crossed 1.43 billion (2023) – world’s largest population
  • Increased demand for food, water, and energy → environmental stress

Case Studies

  • Climate Change: 2022 Pakistan floods affected 33M people
  • Global Warming: 2023 hottest year on record (WMO)
  • Acid Rain: Caused by SO₂ and NOx from industries
  • Ozone Depletion: Montreal Protocol success – ozone hole recovery by 2066
