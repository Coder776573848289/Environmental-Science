About Us

Advancing Environmental Science through Research, Learning, and Awareness

Mission

Our mission is to provide postgraduate students with an academically rigorous, evidence-based, and accessible resource on Environmental Science. We aim to bridge the gap between research knowledge and practical awareness by offering updated data, case studies, and both global as well as Indian perspectives.

Vision

To create a globally recognized educational platform that fosters sustainable thinking, inspires future researchers, and enables evidence-driven policymaking in Environmental Science.

Why This Platform is Needed

  • Global urgency: According to the IPCC (2023), the world has already warmed by 1.1°C above pre-industrial levels, with rising risks of extreme weather events.
  • India’s context: India is the third-largest emitter of CO₂ and ranks among the top 10 most climate-vulnerable countries (Germanwatch, 2022).
  • Educational gap: Many academic resources are fragmented or overly technical. This platform translates research into clear, engaging, and student-focused knowledge.

Next 5-Year Goals

  1. Develop an interactive knowledge hub with updated databases, infographics, and simulation tools.
  2. Collaborate with Indian universities and global NGOs for knowledge exchange.
  3. Launch MOOCs and webinars tailored for postgraduate students and educators.
  4. Publish evidence-based policy briefs for government and research institutions.
  5. Establish a student-led open-access research repository.
