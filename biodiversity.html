Biodiversity & Conservation

Introduction

  • Genetic Diversity: Crop varieties, animal breeds
  • Species Diversity: ~1.7 million species identified globally
  • Ecosystem Diversity: Forests, wetlands, deserts

Value of Biodiversity

  • Consumptive: Food, medicine (50,000+ plant species used medicinally worldwide)
  • Productive: Timber, fisheries
  • Cultural/Ethical: Sacred groves in India
  • Economic: Contributes ~$125 trillion/year to global economy (IPBES, 2019)

Legal Framework

  • Biological Diversity Act (2002)
  • National Biodiversity Authority (NBA): Policy & research
  • State Biodiversity Boards (SBB): Local implementation
  • Biodiversity Management Committees (BMCs): Community involvement
