Natural Resources

Overview

Natural resources are materials or substances that occur in nature and can be used for economic gain and human well-being. They include renewable and non-renewable resources.

Types

  • Renewable: Solar, wind, hydro, biomass
  • Non-renewable: Coal, petroleum, natural gas, minerals
  • Biotic: Forests, wildlife
  • Abiotic: Water, minerals, air

Sustainable Management

  • Reduce, reuse, recycle (3Rs)
  • Conservation practices, afforestation
  • Policy and regulation frameworks
