Natural Resources

Renewable vs Non-renewable

  • Renewable: Solar, wind, forests
  • Non-renewable: Fossil fuels, minerals

Recyclable vs Non-recyclable

  • Recyclable: Paper, metals, glass
  • Non-recyclable: Single-use plastics, radioactive waste

Energy Resources

  • India: 3rd largest energy consumer (IEA, 2022)
  • Renewable capacity: 174 GW (2023), target 500 GW by 2030
  • Case Study: Germany’s Energiewende – 40% electricity from renewables

Role of Individuals

  • Reduce carbon footprint
  • Practice “3Rs” – Reduce, Reuse, Recycle
  • Support eco-friendly products and lifestyle
